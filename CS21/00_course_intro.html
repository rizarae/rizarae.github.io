Course Goals

  1. Understand the fundamentals and design of computers
  2. Identify the connection between hardware and software
  3. Create simple programs using assembly language
  4. Understand, design, and create simple digital circuits
  5. Program microcontrollers and design simple microcontroller-based circuits
