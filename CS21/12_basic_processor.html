Assembly Language: Register Instructions

Assembly Language: Register Instructions

opcode - 6 bits - always 000000

rs - 5 bits - depends on the operand register

rt - 5 bits - depends on the operand register

rd - 5 bits - depends on the destination register

sa - 5 bits - used in shift operations, 00000 otherwise

function - 6 bits - depends on the operation/function

Assembly Language: Immediate Instructions

Assembly Language: Immediate Instructions

opcode - 6 bits - always 000000

rs - 5 bits - depends on the operand register

rt - 5 bits - depends on the operand register

immediate - 16 bits - contains the immediate value

Assembly Language: Jump Instructions

opcode - 6 bits - 000010 for j, 000011 for jal

target - 26 bits - distance of target/destination from current j/jal instruction(in number of instructions)
